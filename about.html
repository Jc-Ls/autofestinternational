🚗About Us

🚗AutoFest International is the premier festival for vehicle enthusiasts — featuring high-octane stunts, curated car exhibitions, live music, food vendors and awards. We bring drivers, makers and fans together for three days of unforgettable experiences.

🚗Our Vision

To be the leading international platform for automotive culture, innovation, and entertainment, inspiring passion and unity among car enthusiasts worldwide.

🚗Our Mission

To create unforgettable experiences by bringing together drivers, makers, fans, and industry leaders through world-class events, stunts, exhibitions, and community engagement.
